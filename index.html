Import from Nexstar Pace Report

How to update daily from the Pace Report Excel:
1. Open your July_2026_Sales_Manager_Pace_Report.xlsx file
2. Go to the sheet for today's date (e.g. "8th")
3. Scroll to the per-rep grid at the bottom of the sheet (below "Calls Ran")
4. Select and copy the rep rows including the header row: columns are
   Rep Name | TGL | TGL Conv | TGL Rev | MKT | MKT Conv | MKT Rev
5. Paste below and click Import Day

Column order expected (tab-separated from Excel copy):
Col 1 — Rep Name
Col 2 — TGL Appointments
Col 3 — TGL Deals Closed
Col 4 — TGL Revenue
Col 5 — MKT Appointments
Col 6 — MKT Deals Closed
Col 7 — MKT Revenue

ServiceTitan Export (Technician Performance)

Alternative: import from ServiceTitan Technician Performance report
1. In ServiceTitan → Reports → Sales → Technician Performance
2. Filter by Business Unit (Sales), set date range to today
3. Expand MKT and TGL columns, use "Close Rate As Of"
4. Copy the data rows and paste below

Expected columns:
Technician — Rep name
MKT Opportunities — MKT Appts
MKT Jobs Sold — MKT Deals
MKT Revenue — MKT Revenue
TGL Opportunities — TGL Appts
TGL Jobs Sold — TGL Deals
TGL Revenue — TGL Revenue
